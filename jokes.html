Christmas Jokes

Christmas is a time for joy and laughter, and what better way to spread cheer than with some good old-fashioned Christmas jokes? Whether you're looking to make your friends and family laugh around the dinner table or just need a little pick-me-up during the hectic holiday season, these festive jokes are sure to bring a smile to your face. So grab a mug of hot chocolate, get comfy by the fire, and enjoy these Christmas jokes that are sure to spread holiday cheer to all. Click on the joke to reveal the answer!

What do you call a penguin in the desert?

Lost.

Why was Santa's little helper feeling sad?

Because he had low elf-esteem.

What do you call an elf that runs away from Santa's workshop?

A rebel without a Claus!

What do snowmen eat for dessert?

Ice crispies.

What do you get when you cross a snowman with a vampire?

Frostbite.

What's every elf's favorite type of music?

Wrap!

What do Santa's elves learn in school?

The elf-abet.

What do you call an obnoxious reindeer?

Rude-olph.

Who is never hungry at Christmas?

The turkey—he's always stuffed.

How much did Santa pay for his sleigh?

Nothing, it was on the house!

What goes “Oh, Oh, Oh”?

Santa walking backward!

What do you get if you cross Santa with a duck?

A Christmas quacker.

How do Christmas trees get ready for a night out?

They spruce up!

Why did the Christmas turkey form a band?

It had the drumsticks!

What did one snowman say to the other snowman?

Does it smell like carrots here?

What do you call a snowman in the desert?

An oasis.

What do reindeer hang on their Christmas trees?

Horn-aments!

Why does The Grinch enjoy gardening?

He's got a green thumb.

Where does Santa store his suit?

In his Claus-et.

Who's the snowman's favorite rapper?

Ice Cube.

Why do reindeer wear bells?

Their horns don't work.
